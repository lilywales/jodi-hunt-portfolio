Manny and the Baby

Cover design for a novel set across two timelines: 1930s London and 2012 Bath. It was crucial to represent the musicality that runs throughout the book, evoking the energy, rhythm, and movement of the underground jazz scene in 1930s London. Using a blend of collage and fluid typography inspired by the bold, expressive styles of that era, I aimed to capture the vibrancy that defines the narrative while showcasing the lively scenes depicted in the story.
