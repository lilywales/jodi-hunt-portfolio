Windrush

These collages explore the legacy of the Windrush
generation and its impact on my identity as a Black British
designer of Jamaican heritage. Inspired by family stories,
archival photographs, and cultural symbols, they celebrate the resilience
and strength of our foreparents, honouring the rich culture they handed
down to us. Initially exhibited on eight 6ft boards, here is a glimpse
into a larger, evolving work that connects deeply with my
journey, featuring poetry by Rider Shafique. We weave our personal
experiences into the rich tapestry of our historybr as descendants of
the Windrush generation. I look forward to pushing
this project to the next level and exhibiting
again soon.

Windrush
